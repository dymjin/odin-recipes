King Ranch Casserole

Description

Traditional texas meal involving corn, chicken, tortillas, tomatoes, and cheese. Hearty meal fit for a special occasion.

Ingredients

  • 1 whole onion
  • 2 bell peppers
  • 1 poblano pepper(for extra heat)
  • 1 canned cream of chicken soup
  • 1 canned cream of mushroom soup
  • 1 can diced tomatoes
  • 1 can green chile peppers
  • 1 carton chicken broth
  • 3 tbsp sour cream
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1 tsp ancho chile powder
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp chipotle chile powder
  • leftover cooked chicken
  • shredded cheddar on top
  • corn tortillas(or tortilla chips for crunch) cut into quarters
